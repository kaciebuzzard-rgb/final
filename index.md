      <p class="section-kicker">About</p>
      <h2>About Kacie</h2>

      <p>
        Kacie Wielgus (Buzzard) is a speaker, writer, and strategist working at the
        intersection of disability advocacy, caregiving systems, and inclusive communications.
      </p>

      <p>
        Drawing on lived experience as a parent navigating complex medical and educational
        systems, her work examines the operational, emotional, and structural realities
        families are expected to carry without acknowledgment or support.
      </p>

      <p>
        Her writing centers on endurance, systems change, and the human stories that exist
        inside healthcare, disability, and caregiving structures.
      </p>

      <p>
        She is currently developing her memoir,
        <em>The Waiting Rooms of My Life</em>.
      </p>
    </div>
